High Quality Untrimmed Hemp Flower

We harvest using the "topping" method to first harvest the best looking and most mature flowers at the beginning of harvest and allowing the bottom of the plant to continue maturing. We generally recommend the top half of the plant for the smokable hemp flower market, and the bottom half for oil processing.

All of our untrimmed flower is "bucked" or "shucked" meaning that all major flowers have been removed from the stem/branch leaving mostly untrimmed flower and sugar leaf. This product is great for creating full spectrum hemp products.
